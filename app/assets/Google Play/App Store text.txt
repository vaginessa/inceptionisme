
Apply the Inceptionism style to your photos. Deep Dream yourself.
---

Choose photos that you would like to run through the Deep Dream neural network.
This will give you the unique style of Inceptionism.
Surprise your friends by deep dreaming their profile pictures.

Deep Dream ME distorts the image and tries to find objects and creatures in it.
What it finds is always surprising and entertaining.
Beautiful, psychedelic, sometimes horrifying.
Go ever deeper by dreaming a photo that has already been dreamed - increasing the strangeness.

The images are not published. It's your own decision to share a photo.
